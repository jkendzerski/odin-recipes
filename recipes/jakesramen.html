Jakes Spicy Ramen

Ramen without the soup. Seems strange but I promise after trying out your favorite 25 cent ramen brand like this you'll never go back to eating your plain ramen soup when you're sick!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of chicken flavored ramen
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • Frank's Red Hot hot sauce

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil ramen packet per regular Instructions
  2. Drain water
  3. Add butter, seasoning packet, mix well
  4. Add Frank's to taste, mix well
