Slutty Brownies

Have you ever wanted to combine brownies, chocolate chip cookies and oreos? If yes then boy do I have a treat for you. This recipe is one of my all time favorites and definitely has put a few pounds on me over the years... I highly recommend.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of brownie mix
  • 1 tub of chocolate chip cookie dough
  • 1 packet of oreos

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit
  2. Oil 9x9 pan
  3. Cover bottom layer in Cookie Dough
  4. For the next layer add your oreos
  5. Cover the rest with the brownie batter
  6. Place on middle rack, bake for ten minutes or until cooked through
  7. Try not to eat them all in one sitting
